Greetings, I am David Lobser. I have an extensive background in 3D animation and I am embarking on a quest to apply my skills to the interactive world. I'm presently a student at NYU's ITP program. Last semester I immersed myself in Processing and Java and this site is the result of my quest to become fluent (or fluent enough to fake it) in javascript and html/css/jquery etc I was planning to stay away from 3D as much as possible but threejs sucked me in. It's a pretty full featured 3D package without the front end, so I can comfortably use tools I'm familiar with while picking up some brand new skills along the way.
